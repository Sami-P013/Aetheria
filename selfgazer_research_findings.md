- **Tagline**: "Have You Met The Mystery That Lives Within You?"
- **Core Philosophy**: Inspired by Jungian psychology for self-discovery
- **Approach**: Integration of esoteric systems and mystical traditions for introspective exploration

### Oracle System Features (Detailed)
The Selfgazer Oracle serves as the central AI companion with four key capabilities:

1. **Personalized Guidance**
   - Tailored insights and interpretations
   - Adapts to unique circumstances, questions, and spiritual journey
   - Creates deeply personal experience

2. **Multi-Tradition Consultation**
   - Access to multiple esoteric traditions (Astrology, Tarot, I Ching)
   - Delivered through comprehensive spiritual narrative
   - Integrated wisdom approach

3. **Psychological Integration**
   - Explores deeper layers of psyche
   - Emotional check-ins for understanding lessons behind experiences
   - Jungian psychology framework

4. **Interactive Exploration**
   - Dynamic conversations
   - Follow-up questions capability
   - Structured dialogue for clarity
   - Dive deeper into specific areas of interest
