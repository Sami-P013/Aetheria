# Integrated Architecture: Selfgazer + Dimensional Platform

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the architectural design for integrating Selfgazer.com's mystical wisdom tools with Dimensional.me's comprehensive personality assessment system. The resulting platform will provide users with a holistic self-discovery experience that bridges ancient wisdom traditions with modern psychological science.

## Core Integration Philosophy

The integration follows a **"Wisdom Meets Science"** approach where:

- **Dimensional assessments** provide the psychological foundation and personality mapping
- **Selfgazer tools** offer mystical insights and archetypal understanding  
- **AI Oracle system** synthesizes both approaches for comprehensive guidance
- **Cross-platform insights** reveal connections between personality traits and mystical archetypes

**Phase 1: Foundation Building**
